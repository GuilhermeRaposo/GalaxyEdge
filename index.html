Galaxy Edge

Warning: sound effects are ON!

HEALTH: 100

SCORE: 0


Instructions :

  • Use left and right arrow keys to move
  • Getting hit by an asteroid makes you lose 50 health
  • Picking up a screwdriver resets your health to 100
  • Picking up a star multiplies the score you get by 3 for 5 seconds
  • Picking up a nuke explodes all the rocks on the screen
  • Survive as long as possible
